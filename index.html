features
EEG Connectivity

EEG connectivity analyzes brain network interactions by measuring electrical activity. It helps understand cognitive functions, diagnose disorders, and assess treatment efficacy through detailed brain signal mapping..

Facial Emotions

Capturing facial emotions involves analyzing expressions using real-time imaging technology to interpret and understand underlying feelings, aiding in mental health assessments and enhancing human-computer interaction

Customize Your Assistant

Customize your voice assistant to meet specific needs: personalize responses, tailor functionality for unique tasks, and enhance user interaction for a more intuitive and efficient experience

Enhanced Spatial Audio

Enhanced spatial audio technology delivers immersive, three-dimensional sound experiences, improving realism and depth in movies, music, and gaming by mimicking natural sound environments accurately

Metahuman and VR Compatibility

Metahuman and VR compatibility enables lifelike digital human creation and interaction within virtual reality environments, enhancing user engagement and realism in simulations, training, and entertainment
